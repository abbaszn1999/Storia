# CLEAR SPACE

Jse x= radius of the orb; keep x clear space around all sides of the logo to ensure visibility and impact. X

# MINIMUM SIZE

Digital: 120px width (wordmark), 24px (orb icon) Print: 20mm width

# LOGO VERSIONS

• Full-color gradient • Solid white (for dark backgrounds) • Solid black (for light backgrounds)

# BACKGROUND VARIATIONS

• On dark (\#1A3A35/\#0B0F12): use white wordmark with gradient orb • On light: use charcoal wordmark with gradient orb; ensure contrast

# FAVICON/APP ICON

Standalone gradient orb centered within square format, 8% safe margin, subtle inner glow effect. Maintain recognizable form at small sizes.

# MISUSE

• Do not stretch or distort the logo • Do not add drop shadows or effects • Do not rotate at angles • Do not use non-approved gradient colors
